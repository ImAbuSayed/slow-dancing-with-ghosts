[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel your shadow moving right across the floor
The scent of midnight creeping in beneath my door
You pull me closer than a memory usually goes
I’m falling deeper into slow dancing with ghosts

[Verse 1]
The embers dying in the fireplace we built
I trace the outlines of your body on the quilt
We left the porch light burning low against the dark
You always knew exactly how to light the spark

[Pre-Chorus]
It’s a quiet kind of fever when you look my way
A heavy kind of silence with so much to say
I taste the summer rain upon your skin tonight
We’re swaying softly in the fading candle light

[Chorus]
Hold me tightly in the spaces where the past still breathes
Wrap your arms around the secrets that the darkness leaves
I can feel your heartbeat racing like a rolling tide
I’m a willing prisoner with you here by my side

[Post-Chorus]
Swirling in the ashes of the love we made
Caught inside a beautiful and endless shade

[Verse 2]
I pour a bourbon just to help the vision stay
I watch you smiling in that subtle, knowing way
Your fingers tracing gentle circles on my chest
You know the places where my heavy head should rest

[Pre-Chorus 2]

[Chorus]

[Post-Chorus 2]

[Bridge]
Oh I need you closer than the air I breathe
Every single whisper is making me believe
Even when you vanish in the morning dew
You know Saeed is waiting in the dark for you
I keep the fire burning as the cold wind blows
Still forever lost in slow dancing with ghosts

[Spoken]
